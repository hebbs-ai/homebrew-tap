# Perplexity AI

## Overview

Perplexity AI is an AI-powered search engine founded in 2022 by Aravind Srinivas, Denis Yarats, Johnny Ho, and Andy Konwinski. The company is headquartered in San Francisco, California.

## Funding

Perplexity raised $73.6 million in Series B funding in April 2024, valuing the company at $1.04 billion. The round was led by IVP and included participation from NEA, Databricks Ventures, and Jeff Bezos.

## Product

Perplexity offers a conversational search engine that provides direct answers with cited sources. It processes over 230 million queries per month as of early 2024. The product is available as a web app, iOS app, and Android app.

## Technology

The system uses a combination of large language models and real-time web indexing. Perplexity Pro subscribers can choose between different models including GPT-4, Claude, and Perplexity's own models.
