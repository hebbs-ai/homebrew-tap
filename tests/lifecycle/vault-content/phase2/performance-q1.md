# Q1 2026 Performance Report

## Revenue Growth

Q1 2026 delivered exceptional revenue growth of 40% year-over-year, reaching $12.4M in quarterly revenue. Enterprise deal closure rate improved to 68%, up from 52% in Q1 2025. The average contract value increased by 25% driven by platform tier upgrades.

## System Reliability

Platform uptime reached 99.95% for the quarter, exceeding our SLO target. Mean time to recovery improved to 4.2 minutes from 12.8 minutes in the previous quarter. Zero customer-impacting incidents were recorded during business hours across all three regions.

## Infrastructure Stability

The infrastructure remained remarkably stable throughout Q1. Kubernetes cluster autoscaling handled traffic surges smoothly with no manual intervention required. Database connection pool utilization stayed below 60% at peak. CDN cache hit ratio averaged 94.2%.

## Team Performance

Engineering velocity hit an all-time high with 32 story points per sprint average. Sprint completion rate was 97%. The team shipped 245 production deployments with a 0.4% rollback rate, indicating high code quality and thorough review processes.
