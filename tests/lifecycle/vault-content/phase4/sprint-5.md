# Sprint 5 Report: Feature Freeze and Stabilization

## Velocity Summary

Sprint 5 delivered only 14 story points out of 24 planned (58% completion rate). This represents the lowest velocity in 12 months. The team is in stabilization mode with a feature freeze imposed by engineering leadership.

## Key Deliverables

Fixed 12 high-priority bugs identified during the Q2 stability review. Implemented circuit breakers for all external service calls. Added retry logic with exponential backoff for the payment processing pipeline.

## Blockers

Three concurrent production incidents in week 1 consumed 60% of the team's capacity. A second senior engineer submitted their resignation, citing burnout from excessive on-call load. The hiring pipeline has no candidates past the phone screen stage.
