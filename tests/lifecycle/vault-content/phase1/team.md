# Engineering Team Structure

## Leadership

Alice Chen is the VP of Engineering, overseeing all technical teams. She reports directly to the CTO and manages a team of 45 engineers across four divisions. Alice joined NovaTech from Google in 2023 and brought a strong focus on reliability engineering.

Bob Martinez leads the Backend Team as Principal Engineer. He has 15 years of distributed systems experience and designed the current microservices architecture.

## Backend Team

The backend team consists of 12 engineers working in two squads. Squad Alpha focuses on core platform services (auth, billing, notifications). Squad Beta handles data pipeline and analytics infrastructure. Sprint velocity averages 28 story points per two-week sprint.

Carol Kim is the tech lead for Squad Alpha. David Park leads Squad Beta. Both report to Bob Martinez.

## Frontend Team

The frontend team has 8 engineers led by Senior Staff Engineer Elena Rodriguez. They maintain the customer portal, admin dashboard, and internal tools. The team recently completed a migration from Create React App to Next.js, reducing initial load time by 60%.

## Infrastructure Team

The infrastructure team of 6 engineers is led by Frank Wilson. They manage Kubernetes clusters across three AWS regions, maintain the CI/CD pipeline, and operate the monitoring stack (Datadog, PagerDuty). On-call rotation covers 24/7 with a 15-minute response SLA.

## Velocity and Delivery

The organization delivers on a two-week sprint cadence with quarterly planning. Average cycle time from ticket creation to production is 4.2 days. The team maintains a 95% sprint completion rate and ships approximately 200 production deployments per month.
